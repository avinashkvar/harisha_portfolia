I'm a passionate Mechanical Design Engineer with extensive experience in the Space and Defence electronics industry. My expertise lies in creating innovative mechanical packaging solutions for complex RF, Power, and Digital systems.

Throughout my career at Centum Electronics Ltd, I've been responsible for designing and developing customized mechanical systems that meet stringent industry standards including JSS55555, MIL STD, and ISRO specifications. I specialize in thermal management solutions, structural analysis, and ensuring EMI/EMC compliance.

My educational background includes a BE in Mechanical Engineering with a CGPA of 8.5, and I'm proficient in multiple languages including English and Kannada. I'm constantly learning and adapting to new technologies to deliver cutting-edge solutions.
